Substring-Search Algorithms:

from Tushar Roy videos on youtube

1) Brute Force Search O(mn)
2) KMP algorithm (Knuth-Morris-Pratt) O(m+n)
3) Z algorithm O(m+n)
4) Rabin Karp (compare rolling hash to pattern hash) O(mn)
5) Boyer-Moore (compare ends of strings) O(mn)
6) Aho-Corasick
7) Finate state automation
